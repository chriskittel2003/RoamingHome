About the Band

Roaming Home is a high-energy three-piece rock band from Belleville, Illinois, serving the greater St. Louis bi-state region. Known for tight musicianship, powerful performances, and engaging crowd interaction, the band delivers fresh takes on classic rock favorites alongside compelling original songs.

Their live shows are electric—featuring blues-inspired arrangements and infectious energy that gets audiences on their feet. Whether playing intimate venues, wineries, bars, or community events, Roaming Home brings the passion and professionalism that defines great rock music.
